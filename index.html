Delicious Poha Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

  • Poha (Flattened Rice): 1.5 cups (114 grams)
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons (30 ml)
  • Mustard Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Curry Leaves: 8 to 12 (medium to large sized)
  • Green Chilies: 2 to 3 (finely chopped)
  • Onions: 1 medium (finely chopped)
  • Turmeric Powder: ¼ teaspoon
  • Salt: ¾ teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Coriander Leaves (Cilantro): 2 to 3 tablespoons (chopped)
  • Peanuts: 3 to 4 tablespoons (roasted)

Preparation

  1. Rinse 2–3 cups of poha in water and let it soak for 3–4 minutes.
  2. Drain the water from the rice.
  3. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil on medium in a large wok or saucepan.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds and let them crackle.
  5. Add the diced onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  6. Add curry leaves, turmeric, salt, and roasted peanuts. Stir everything together.
  7. Add the soaked poha and stir gently to mix with the spices.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve hot.
